Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ¼ tabelspoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instruction

  1. Take a bowl and add flour, sugar, milk and butter
  2. Mix all the ingredients well
  3. Take a pan and add the mixture in it
  4. Now cook it for 10 minutes
  5. Take a plate and add the mixture in it
  6. Add cinnamon powder and sugar
  7. Now roll it
  8. Cut it into pieces
  9. Serve it and enjoy
